Veg Upma Full Recipe

Description

Vegetable Upma is a quick and healthy South Indian breakfast made from semolina (sooji), lightly roasted and cooked with vegetables, spices, and herbs. It's soft, savory, and perfect for a light meal or snack.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup semolina (sooji/rava)
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 green chili, chopped (optional)
  • 1 small carrot, chopped
  • 5-6 beans, chopped (or any veggie you have)
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp urad dal (optional)
  • 8-10 curry leaves (optional)
  • 2 cups water
  • Salt, to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves, chopped (for garnish)
  • Lemon juice (optional)

Step by step instructions

  1. In a dry pan, roast 1 cup semolina on medium heat until it turns light golden and aromatic (4-5 minutes).
  2. Remove it from the pan and set aside.
  3. In the same pan, heat 2 tbsp oil.
  4. Add mustard seeds, and let them splutter.
  5. (Optional) Add urad dal and curry leaves.
  6. Add chopped onions and sauté until translucent.
  7. Add chopped carrots, beans, and green chili. Sauté for 2-3 minutes.
  8. Add 2 cups of water and salt to the pan. Bring to a boil.
  9. Slowly add the roasted semolina, stirring constantly to avoid lumps.
  10. Cook for 2-3 minutes, stirring until it thickens and the water is absorbed.
  11. Turn off the heat, cover for a few minutes.
  12. Garnish with chopped coriander and a little lemon juice if you like.

Serve hot as a Light meal or Breakfast
